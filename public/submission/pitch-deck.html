AgriBatch Pay: problem and solution

Crop settlement is slow to reconcile when batches, quality checks, and payouts live in separate tools.

AgriBatch Pay links farmer lots, buyer funding, cooperative approvals, and payout evidence.

Market opportunity

Agricultural cooperatives and buyers need transparent settlement rails across fragmented batch workflows.

Architecture

Next.js operations UI → Neon persistence → Soroban registry/vault → wallet and realtime event proof.

Growth strategy

Land cooperatives first, then buyer networks; use feedback to improve proof visibility and batch status clarity.

Future roadmap

Add recurring settlements, quality-provider integrations, multi-currency support, and partner reporting.
