Problem02 / 12

Crop settlement slows down when batch quality and payout proof are disconnected.

Farmer

Needs a clear record of lot status and what is required before payout.

Buyer

Needs confidence that funding tracks the correct batch and approval state.

Cooperative

Needs one operational view for quality confirmation and settlement oversight.

Separate spreadsheets, messages, and payment records make it hard to reconcile a batch from delivery through payment.

03

04

Release payout

Settlement evidence remains connected to the batch.
