AgriBatch Pay

Stellar-powered crop settlement for cooperatives, buyers, and farmers.

Problem

Batch payouts are slow, opaque, and hard to audit.

Farmer lots, quality checks, and buyer funding often live in separate tools, making settlement proof painful.

Solution

One operational workspace for batch settlement.

Create crop batches and farmer lots.
Connect Freighter or Rabet wallets.
Invoke Soroban registry and payout vault flows.
Track live events, health, and submission proof.
